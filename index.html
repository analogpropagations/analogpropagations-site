Radio Garden

A spinning globe. Every dot a live radio station. Click one somewhere in coastal Japan at midnight and you're there — a DJ talking between songs in a language you don't speak, weather you'll never feel, music you'd never have found. Radio Garden is technically an app but the experience is something older. Presence without travel. Discovery without an algorithm deciding what you're ready for.

Three worth finding: FIP Paris  ·  Ashiya Radio, Japan  ·  Pan African Space Station
